About Me

I’m a Data Analyst with over two years of experience transforming operational, customer, and revenue data into clear, actionable insights that support smarter business decisions. I specialize in SQL, Excel, Power BI, and Python, and I work across the full analytics lifecycle—from data cleaning and validation to dashboard development, KPI monitoring, and trend analysis.

My work focuses on helping teams understand performance, uncover improvement opportunities, and communicate insights in a way that is simple, visual, and decision-ready. I have analyzed thousands of records, built interactive dashboards, reduced reporting errors, and improved reporting efficiency—always with the goal of making data more reliable and easier to act on.

I’m particularly interested in business intelligence, product analytics, and customer experience insights, and I enjoy collaborating with cross-functional teams to translate real business questions into meaningful analysis. I’m continuously expanding my skills in analytics, statistics, and modern data tools, with the long-term goal of growing into a senior analytics or insights role within a global, data-driven organization.
